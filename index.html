Build your war base
One of the most important parts to any War World is the war system! War World II is changing the War System in attempt to close the gaps of the problems with past war systems.

This system allows teams to gain something from war, while ensuring the other team doesn't lose anything of large value, along with making wars more entertaining and realistic.

War bases are usually better defended, and contain a core in the center of the base which if destroyed causes the attacker to win the battle.
War Bases
War bases are seperate bases you create from your main bases and cities that are the only bases you can lose in war. War bases include your military headquarters, military outposts, and any other base that is fit for a military base.
Progressive Wars
When teams declare war, they can have one battle, or if the other team feels that they still have a chance, they can endure many more battles, with light bombings or sabotages during war being allowed on any base so long as the damage is light.
Progression Evolved
Progression has received a massive overhaul in War World II, with Team progression changing to be more flexible and easy, and a brand-new type of progression to liven up the world with a civilian population!

Cities and Teams now progress by population! Cities are bases where civillians live or bases with multiple bases where troops live, either are considered as cities. Civillians are simply villigars and live in cities.
